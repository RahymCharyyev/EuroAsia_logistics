Providing railcars for loading

Our company provides services for the provision of various types of rolling stock for loading. Our team of specialists has the necessary skills and knowledge to provide a quality service. We offer a flexible pricing policy and an individual approach to each client.

Customs clearance

Customs clearance is an important stage in the process of international trade. Our professionals will help you to correctly prepare the necessary documentation and resolve any issues related to customs control. We offer services for the registration of import and export cargo, consultation on customs regulations, and assistance in document preparation. With us, you can be sure that your cargo shipment will pass customs control without delays or problems.

International transportation

Our company provides professional services for international transportation of goods of any type and volume. We work with clients from different countries and guarantee fast and safe delivery of your cargo to any point in the world. Our team of experienced specialists takes care of every step of transportation, including document preparation, optimal route selection and location information throughout the journey. We value every client and always strive to find the best solution for their individual needs. Contact us and see for yourself the high quality of our services.

Route Adjustment

Our route adjustment service allows our customers to quickly and flexibly respond to changes in plans. If you encounter unexpected circumstances or need to change your route, we will help you organize the new route as efficiently as possible. Our experienced specialists will take all your requirements into account and find the best route option, taking into account all factors such as distance, time, and cost. Contact us and we will do our best to make your trip as comfortable and safe as possible.

Cargo Location Report

Our cargo location report service will help you always stay informed about the location of your cargo in real time. We provide our clients with complete information about the location of the cargo at each stage of its movement, as well as any changes in the delivery schedule. You can receive reports at a convenient time and in the format of your choice to have full control over your cargoes. Our team of specialists is ready to answer any questions related to cargo tracking and provide you with the most up-to-date information about the location of your cargo at any time.

Cargo Escorting

We provide a cargo escorting service to ensure maximum safety and protection of your cargo throughout the journey. Our team of specialists works closely with rail agencies to ensure that the goods are handled, transported and delivered correctly. We monitor every stage of the cargo movement, from loading to unloading, and provide detailed reports on each step of the process. We are also ready to respond to any unforeseen circumstances that may arise during the transportation of the cargo to timely resolve the problem and minimize any potential risks. Contact us to get high-quality cargo escorting.

About us

"Eurasia Logistics" company, with vast experience, provides transportation of goods and a wide range of services related to the use of railway transport across the territories of Central Asia and the CIS. To ensure high quality services, the company has permanent offices in regional centers of Turkmenistan, such as Ahal, Mary, Balkan (Balkanabat city, Turkmenbashi port, Hazar), Lebap, Dashoguz. Being the official freight forwarder of the railway administration of Turkmenistan based on a cooperation agreement for the transportation of goods in international railway communication, "Eurasia Logistics" provides its customers with high-quality services, competitive rates and up-to-date information. Our company closely cooperates with partners from Turkey, UAE, China, Russia, Kazakhstan, Uzbekistan, Azerbaijan, Georgia and other countries of Central Asia, the CIS, which enables us to quickly and efficiently provide all types of transportation and logistics services both in Turkmenistan and beyond.
